Study Abroad Education Landing Page Template

A modern template for education agencies or platforms to showcase European university application services, student guidance, and study abroad workflows.

Education Bridge is a polished landing page template for showcasing study-abroad and university admissions services focused on Europe.

What’s Included
Hero section with bold headline, social proof, and dual CTAs
Animated visual cards for admissions, visa, and document status
Value proposition grid, stats, and real-time tracking feature split
Team/mentor profiles with interactive modal and chat/call CTAs
Conversion-focused CTA section and structured footer
Key Features
Modern, clean Tailwind CSS layout with gradients and large type
Program search, unified application, and mentorship storytelling baked into the copy
Stats and trust indicators to boost credibility
Responsive, mobile-friendly structure ready for customization
Clear primary CTAs (“Get Started”, “Apply Now”) to drive signups
Ideal for education platforms, agencies, or consultants helping students apply to European universities, this template speeds up launch while leaving room for brand customization.
