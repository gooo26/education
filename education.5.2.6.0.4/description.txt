AzubiPro Education Landing Page Template

AzubiPro Education Landing Page Template is a sleek, dark-mode one-pager built for exam-prep and training platforms, especially in insurance and finance.

What’s Included
Hero with bold headline, social proof and dual CTAs
Feature sections for learning platform, mobile learning and oral exam prep
Detailed pricing grid with highlight “Komplettpaket” offer
Training/“Schulungen” section with visual calendar
FAQ and footer with legal, support and account links
Key Features
Conversion-focused CTAs (“Pakete ansehen”, “Jetzt kaufen”) and package tiers
Education-first storytelling for self-learning and live trainings
Responsive, mobile-ready layout with modern dark, gradient styling
Clear information architecture for Pakete, Schulungen, FAQ, Login
Brandable structure aligned to Azubi-style exam brands
Use this template to launch or prototype a professional exam preparation or e-learning landing page fast, then customize copy, pricing, and branding to your own education product.
