Editucation Motion Design Landing Page Template

A high-converting, dark-theme landing page template built to sell motion design or creative education programs.

Key features
Clean, premium dark UI with accent color for CTAs

Primary use case
Use this template to launch or relaunch a motion design course, mentorship program, or creative education offer with a narrative-driven, conversion-focused landing page.
