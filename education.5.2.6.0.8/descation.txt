Vertex Online Learning Landing Page Template

A dark, futuristic landing page template for high-performance online education platforms focused on AI, design, and engineering.

What’s Included
Hero with bold headline, social proof badge, and dual CTAs
Platform feature section with timeline, imagery, and secondary CTA
Integrations showcase with animated network graphic
Courses grid for featured programs and durations
Community + marquee gallery, testimonial, and conversion-focused footer
Key Features
Conversion-focused hero with “Start Learning” and “Browse Catalog” CTAs
Course catalog grid optimized for categories, lessons, and timings
Community-centric sections to highlight peers and social proof
Modern dark UI with gradients, motion, and glowing accents
Responsive Tailwind-based layout ready for customization
Primary Use Case
Ideal for teams launching an online learning, cohort-based course, or AI/tech education brand who need a high-impact landing page template that drives enrollments and community signups.
