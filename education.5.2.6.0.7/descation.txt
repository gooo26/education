Instagram Carousel Post Template

A dark, minimalist Instagram carousel template for 1080x1080 strategy content, ready to customize and reuse for client education and brand storytelling.

Resonance Carousel is a dark, minimal 1080x1080 Instagram carousel template designed for strategy-led content and client education.

What's Included
Four-slide square carousel structure (hook, problem, insight, conclusion)
Bold serif headlines with clean sans-serif body copy
Dark, high-contrast aesthetic with emerald accents
Built-in pagination indicators and swipe prompt
Space for handle/credit and subtle branding
Key Features
Story-arc flow: Hook, misconception, insight, solution mapped slide by slide.
Optimized for readability with large type and tight hierarchy.
Brand-ready layout for agencies and consultants.
Easy customization of text, icons, and colors.
On-brand visuals using gradients, grids, and icons.
Primary use case: reuse this template to quickly produce polished Instagram carousels that explain complex services (like automation and workflows) in a way customers actually understand and value.
